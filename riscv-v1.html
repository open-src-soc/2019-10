Premières Rencontres RISC-V – First RISC-V Meetings

The first “RISC-V Meetings” on the impact and opportunities of RISC-V were dedicated to “Accademic RISC-V opportunities.”

They took place on Wednesday October 17th, in CEA's premises, at Minatec Grenoble, France.

The program of this first edition is available online. A vidéo summary was presented at HiPEAC CSW 2018.
